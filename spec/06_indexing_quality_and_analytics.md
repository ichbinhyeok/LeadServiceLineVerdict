# 06 Indexing Quality And Analytics

## Indexing principle
Generate broadly, index selectively.

## Index by default
- Utility pages with a current official inventory or replacement program
- State hub pages with enough downstream depth

## Index selectively
- City pages that materially change the answer
- Cost pages with grounded local responsibility logic
- Testing and filter pages in high-uncertainty markets

## Noindex rules
- Pages without an official local source
- City pages fully subsumed by a utility page
- Thin pages with no action path

## Quality gates
- At least one official local source
- A visible `last verified` date
- A distinct local decision block
- A clear next-step CTA
- No unresolved conflict between public-side and private-side logic

## Core analytics
- Organic landings by template type
- CTA clicks by line-status route
- Utility page performance versus city page performance
- Source freshness coverage
- Quote or consult conversion rate by page family

## Kill rules
- Indexed utility pages with no impression growth across the seeded cohort
- City pages repeatedly cannibalized by their governing utility page
- High-impression pages with no CTA interaction after title and CTA iteration
